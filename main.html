Summary

"3 Idiots" is a story about three engineering students who share a strong bond of friendship. The movie highlights the pressure of the education system and encourages students to follow their passion instead of blindly chasing success. Rancho inspires his friends to think differently and live life with confidence.

Actors

The movie stars Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, Sharman Joshi as Raju, Kareena Kapoor as Pia, and Boman Irani as the strict college director Virus.

Favorite Quote

"All is well" – A simple phrase that teaches us to stay calm and positive even in difficult situations.
