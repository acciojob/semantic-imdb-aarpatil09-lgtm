Summary

3 Idiots is a story about three engineering students who challenge the traditional education system. The movie shows friendship, pressure of studies, and the importance of following passion instead of just running behind success.

Actors

The movie stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi in lead roles. Kareena Kapoor also plays an important role in the film.
