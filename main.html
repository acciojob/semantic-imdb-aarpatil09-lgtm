Summary

3 Idiots is a story about three engineering students who form a strong friendship. The movie highlights the pressure of education systems and encourages learning with passion instead of just chasing marks. It shows how following your dreams is more important than blindly following societal expectations.

Actors

The movie stars Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, Sharman Joshi as Raju, Kareena Kapoor as Pia, and Boman Irani as the strict college director Virus.
